## My Recommendation

**For Feature Graphic (Top Slogan):**
- **Option 1** or **Option 5** - Short, impactful, works well visually

**For Store Listing (Short Description):**
- **Option 4** or **Option 6** - More descriptive, explains value

**For Tagline (Below App Name):**
- **Option 7** - Matches your current "Your Productivity Companion" style

---

## Usage Tips

1. **Feature Graphic**: Use shorter slogans (Option 1, 5, or 7)
2. **Store Listing**: Use descriptive slogans (Option 4 or 6)
3. **Consistency**: Use the same slogan across all marketing materials
4. **Localization**: Ensure Chinese translations are culturally appropriate
