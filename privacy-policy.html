Privacy Policy for Pocket Finance

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Pocket Finance ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

Our app allows you to track your finances by entering:

  • Expense records
  • Asset information
  • Balance sheet data
  • Financial reports and analytics

Important: All financial data you enter is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We do not transmit, upload, or store this data on external servers.

2.2 Data Collected by Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID, iOS Identifier for Advertisers)
  • Location data (approximate location based on IP address)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data
  • Advertising interaction data

This data collection is performed by Google AdMob in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have direct access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob's use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

3.2 Google Play Billing

If you make in-app purchases (such as removing ads), the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment information. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our app's functionality
  • To display relevant advertisements through AdMob
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To improve our app's performance and user experience

5. Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This data:

  • Remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers
  • Is protected by your device's security features
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app on your device
  • Delete: Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
